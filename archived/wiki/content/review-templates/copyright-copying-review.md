# Copyright/Copying Review Checklist

Use this checklist before publishing or promoting article maturity.

- Confirm WorkSafeBC, BCCSA, CSA, manufacturer, and private manual text has not been copied heavily.
- Use official section names and short required terms only where needed for citation clarity.
- Do not import private company procedures or present them as public standards.
- Keep source notes paraphrased and link to official public sources.
- Keep any direct quotation brief, necessary, and traceable to a public official source.
