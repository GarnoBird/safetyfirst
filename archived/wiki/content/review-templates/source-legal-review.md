# Source/Legal Review Checklist

Use this checklist before changing an article from `Draft` to `Source checked`.

- Confirm every legal requirement is supported by an exact official citation or remains marked with `{{review:source}}`.
- Confirm guideline, industry, owner, manufacturer, and best-practice material is not presented as law.
- Confirm source notes and article citations point to current public sources.
- Confirm article wording does not overstate legal certainty or remove required professional judgement.
- Do not mark the article `Source checked` while any `{{review:source}}` flag remains.
