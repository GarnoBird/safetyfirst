# BC Construction Safety Wiki Content

The running prototype currently renders wiki content from `src/wikiContent.js`.
This folder documents the intended long-term Markdown/MDX structure for moving
the wiki into a source-controlled editorial workflow.

Planned structure:

```text
content/
  articles/
  glossary/
  redirects/
  templates/
  checklists/
  procedures/
  source-notes/
```

Rules:

- Write original plain-language summaries.
- Cite official sources for every legal requirement.
- Label legal requirements, best practices, sample procedures, and field
  checklists separately.
- Do not copy private company manuals, CSA/ANSI standards, manufacturer manuals,
  or long passages from public sources.
- Human safety/source review is required before public launch.
