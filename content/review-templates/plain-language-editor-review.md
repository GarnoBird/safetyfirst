# Plain-Language Editor Review Checklist

Use this checklist before sending an article to qualified source or field review.

- Confirm the summary reads like a human safety reference, not a generated placeholder.
- Confirm aliases, redirects, glossary terms, and related topics use worker and supervisor language.
- Confirm legal requirement, best practice, sample procedure, and checklist sections stay separate.
- Confirm the article avoids company-specific fluff and unnecessary personal-information collection.
- Confirm mobile reading and print-friendly checklist use are still practical.
