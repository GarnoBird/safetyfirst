# Field Safety Review Checklist

Use this checklist before changing an article from `Draft` or `Source checked` to `Safety reviewed`.

- Confirm the safe procedure can be followed by a BC construction supervisor or worker at the work face.
- Confirm worker and supervisor checklists match the article hazards and controls.
- Confirm stop-work triggers, emergency escalation, and adjacent-trade/public interface controls are practical.
- Confirm required documents are realistic for the task and stage of work.
- Confirm related toolbox talks, checklists, quizzes, forms, and safety packs are useful for the topic.
